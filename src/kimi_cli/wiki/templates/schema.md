# Wiki Schema

This Wiki stores durable, high-value knowledge as Markdown.

## Categories

- `entities/` — people, organizations, products, and places.
- `concepts/` — definitions, frameworks, and ideas.
- `comparisons/` — structured alternatives and trade-offs.
- `sources/` — concise source summaries.
- `queries/` — reusable answers and synthesis.
- `lint/` — health-check reports.

## Page format

Pages use YAML frontmatter and Markdown. Link related pages with `[[category/page]]`.
Keep claims attributable to their sources, preserve uncertainty, and do not store credentials,
private secrets, machine-specific paths, or full conversation transcripts.
